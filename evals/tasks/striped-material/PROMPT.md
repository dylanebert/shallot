# A cube with a moving pattern on it

I want a cube whose surface has a pattern running across it — think stripes or bands — and I'd like
that pattern to keep moving on its own, so the cube always looks alive. A single flat colour won't do:
I want to actually see lines or bands on the surface, and I want them to shift over time.

The camera can stay where it is. It's the pattern on the cube that should be moving.
