Anamorphic lens flare. Projects the sun direction (from `Sun` or the active `DirectionalLight`) to screen space, draws ghost reflections along the sun-to-center axis with chromatic dispersion, halo, and starburst diffraction. Per-camera component drives intensity, ghost count, dispersal, halo radius, chromatic, and starburst.
